Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are very intelligent birds
  • Pigeons may have been the first bird that was domesticated by humans
  • Pidgeons can find their way back to their nest from 1300 miles away

Interesting Facts About Bears

  • Bears have excellent navigation skills
  • Bears have a very good sense of smell and hearing
  • Bears mainly eat meat and fish

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Pandas need at least two different types of bamboo to avoid starvation
  • An adult panda weighs an average of 100 pounds
  • Giant pandas are very good swimmers

Interesting Facts About Flying Cicadas

  • Flying cicadas can survive huge falls as babies
  • Most cicadas have red or orange eyes but some have blue or gray eyes
  • Male cicadas make loud buzzing noises when they flap their wings
